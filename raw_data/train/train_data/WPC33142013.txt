            Heard Mr. Bhaskar Dev Konwar, learned senior counsel assisted by Ms.
    Jyotimala Konwar, learned counsel appearing for the petitioner. Also heard Mr. Narayan
    Sarma, learned Standing Counsel, Education Department for the Respondent Nos. 1 and
    2, Mr. Chandra Baruah, learned Standing Counsel, APSC, for the Respondent No. 3 and
    Mr. Jyotirmoy Sarmah, learned counsel for the respondent No. 4.
            The petitioner, Senior Lecturer, Government Banikanta College of Teacher
    Education, Guwahati while holding the charge of Principal-in-Charge of said College, the
    respondent No. 1 Commissioner & Secretary to the Government of Assam Elementary
    Education Department by the impugned order dated 10.06.2013 allowed the respondent
    No. 4, Professor of said College to hold the charge of Principal of said Banikanta College
    of Teacher Education, Guwahati.
            Being aggrieved with the same, the petitioner preferred this petition. The court
    while issuing notice to the respondents in the present proceeding, in the interim passed
    an order of status quo as on 17.06.2013 on the post of in-charge Principal in said
    college.
            In the meanwhile the petitioner retired from service on attaining the age of
    superannuation w.e.f. 26.11.2015 and as such nothing survives to adjudicate in this writ
    petition and accordingly stands closed.
